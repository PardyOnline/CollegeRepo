Welcome to the Voodoo Rooms Cork

Calling all students, over 23s or anyone who loves a night out in a premier nightclub!
Voodoo offers some of the best nightlife in Cork City, with VIP booths, amazing deals on drinks, 3 floors and incredible DJs, this is most definitely the nightclub for you.

Make sure not to miss our Tuesday nights if you're a student who loves cheap drink! It's a must-see as we run a Wall Street event where every drink could come down in price at any second, so keep your eyes open! Not fancying a pint for 5 euro? Maybe have a double vodka and coke for the reduced price of 3.90! But come quick as the price will skyrocket again soon!

Also make sure not to miss our Thursday nights if you're a student, as Voodoo is the ultimate experience of Cork nightlife.

Have a birthday or other celebration coming up? Head over to our Bookings page and make Voodoo your celebration destination!

We hope to see you soon! Do you Voodoo?
